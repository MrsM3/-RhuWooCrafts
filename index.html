RhuWoo Crafts

Bespoke book sculptures and crafts for the ideal gift.

Welcome to RhuWoo; the beautifully hand-crafted folded book art store.

All orders are bespoke and customised to your specific requirements. You decide the final look of each design, so that your gift/home decoration has the personal touches that will guarantee to amaze and delight those who receive it.

There are so many books that are 'unloved' or 'unread' and together we will give these books a second chance at life. You are breathing new life into these books by turning them into a personally designed decoration or gift. These gifts are not only unique but also distinctive in the fact that there is only one of its kind - one of a kind that is imagined by you.

Each book is brand-new and in excellent condition. The hardcovers have no stains, or rips or have not been damaged in any way. The internal pages will be newly folded, crisp and contain no foxing (the age-related process of deterioration that causes spots and browning).

I look forward to working with you to achieve the perfect gift for those that you appreciate and love.
